Stained Glass Gallery

Welcome to my stained glass work! In the Spring 2025 semester I took an 8 week course on Stained Glass at the Pittsburgh Glass Center. This started my obsession, and I have since completed several projects in a converted office/studio space in my apartment. Below you'll find my recent works with some basic details-enjoy!.

Hammerhead Shark

Date Completed
September 2025
Pattern Credit
Custom w/ Inspiration from Curiousity Designs
Technique
Copper foil, black patina finish
Dimensions
Roughly 9x5"

Another gift for a friend! Here I custom made the design in procreate, printed it on vinyl and used that to cut my shapes. I used 3 different glass types and used black patina. I also made my own jump rings for the first time here for hanging. I just hope they are sturdy enough to last!

Dragonfly

Date Completed
June 2025
Pattern Credit
Delphi Glass
Technique
Copper foil, no patina finish
Dimensions
Roughly 7" diameter

For this project, I used vinyl and a secondhand cricut machine to make the pattern. This really helped in the cutting and grinding stages. I used 4 different glass types, and after a vote between my friends, I decided not to use patina.
