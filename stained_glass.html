Stained Glass Gallery

Welcome to my stained glass journey. What started as a creative outlet has become a passionate exploration of light, color, and craftsmanship. Each piece represents hours of careful planning, cutting, and soldering, transforming sheets of colored glass into luminous works of art. Below you'll find my recent projects, each with its own story and creative challenges.
