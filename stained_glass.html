Stained Glass Gallery

Welcome to my stained glass works! In the Spring 2025 semester I took an 8 week course at the Pittsburgh Glass Center. This started my endeavor into the art form, and I have since completed several projects in a converted office/studio space in my apartment. Below you'll find my recent projects, each with its own story and creative challenges.

Dragonfly

Date Completed
June 2025
Pattern Credit
Delphi Glass
Technique
Copper foil, no patina finish
Dimensions
Roughly 7" diameter

For this project, I used vinyl and a secondhand cricut machine to make the pattern. This really helped in the cutting and grinding stages. I used 4 different glass types, and after a vote between my friends, I decided not to use patina.

Bird

Date Completed
June 2025
Pattern Credit
KamillaArtPatterns on Etsy
Technique
Copper foil with black patina
Dimensions
Roughly 3x3"

A moving gift for a friend, this piece was supposed to be a bluejay, but I accidenatlly made it into a male bluebird. The size of this pattern was much smaller than I originally thought, making the small pieces hard to handle

Tulip

Date Completed
May 2025
Pattern Credit

Technique
